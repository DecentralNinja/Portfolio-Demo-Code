Askari IV, Karachi — Complete Guide for Overseas Pakistanis

1. Overview

Askari IV is a highly secure, army-managed apartment complex in Karachi, ideal for families and overseas Pakistanis looking for a safe and comfortable living space.

2. Location

  • Main Rashid Minhas Road, near NIPA Chowrangi and Millennium Mall
  • Located on the Gulshan-e-Iqbal side of Karachi
  • Central for airport (20–25 min), Saddar/Cantt (20 min), and major hospitals/universities

3. Apartment Types

  • Mostly 3-bedroom units (~1400–2000 sq.ft.)
  • Some 2-bedroom units available
  • Features drawing/dining rooms, kitchens, balconies

4. Security and Community

  • Fully boundary-walled with gated entry
  • 24/7 armed guards
  • Army-managed with strict tenant rules (no bachelors)
  • Family-friendly, well-lit environment

5. Amenities and Features

  • Parks and playgrounds
  • Walking/jogging tracks
  • On-site mosque
  • Grocery stores nearby
  • Dedicated parking spaces
  • Own water boring + KWSB backup supply
  • Regular cleaning and garbage disposal
  • Reliable fiber internet access

9. Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Excellent security
  • Family-oriented community
  • Central location
  • Reliable utilities + backup power
  • Good resale/rental value

Cons

  • No bachelor tenants allowed
  • Higher rent compared to other Karachi areas
  • Strict society rules on visitors and parking
